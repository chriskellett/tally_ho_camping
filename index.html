A place to escape

Adventure camping in Pembrokeshire.

Get back to nature with Tally Ho adventure camping.

Eight individual pitches set in 3 glorious acres of unspoilt meadowland with certain pitches enjoying pool views; watch nature at its best from your very own piece of beautiful Pembrokeshire. If getting back to nature in a Covid safe way is your thing, then Tally Ho is the place for you.

Tally Ho is a chilled campsite, aiming at never being crowded, ideal for a couple or family to enjoy a bit of a wild campsite experience.

Meet your hosts

Will and Heidi would like to welcome you to Tally Ho adventure camping and Pembrokeshire. Pembrokeshire is the best outdoor play area for the adventurous, lots of walking, swimming, rambling, cycling, horse riding, Pembrokeshire really does have it all, not to mention the amazing beaches and stunning coastal paths.

Tally Ho is situated in the very heart of Pembrokeshire countryside, sitting between Haverfordwest and Pembroke.

Access to the nearest beach is about 10 minutes, St Davids 15 miles.  
Set in a secluded setting, enjoy the peace and tranquillity that the countryside has to offer.  

Tally Ho is a small working holding enjoying 40 acres of space consisting of meadowland, pools and woodland.
